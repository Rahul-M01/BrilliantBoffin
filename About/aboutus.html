About Us

About The Institute

Brilliant Boffin is a child enrichment program where children are treated as the scientists of tomorrow. They explore, experiment, interact. Tailor-made sessions, hands-on creative ways spark their curiosity. Children are guided by a teacher who shares her scientific curiosity and believes in the power of education. Scientific vocabulary, Observation, conclusion skills are developed though age appropriate well crafted session.

OUR MISSION- At Brilliant Boffin, we make science a way of thinking for children through sparking the interest, igniting the love for learning and innovative hands of experiments which make learning an unforgettable experience.

THE MODULES- This is the pulse of what Brilliant Boffin has to offer. The Modules are a structured program consisting of weekly classes, taught in a small group setting. They give context to learning, by tying important scientific concepts together into an exciting overarching story, told through hands-on science. Students sign up for the term, typically 10 to 12 classes, and attend one class a week. The Modules are designed for ages 4 and up, but are divided into 3 levels which are suitable for specific age ranges. All levels follow our constructivist approach but focus on different aspects of the Scientific Method. Observe, for ages 4–6 , Evolve, for ages 7 to 9 and Explore, for ages 9-12.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our talented workshop demonstrators are experienced in teaching science in Primary school and early childhood learning centers. Some are qualified teachers and some are trained scientists with a specific science streams such as physical, life or earth science.

A personalized presenter for your class or group, setup and cleanup of the venue, all materials and equipment, a certificate for each child, a PD certificate for each teacher/supervisor, and further DIY activity ideas.

Rest assured, our sessions are completely safe. We only choose the safest of activities without compromising on learning.

We currently operate within primary schools and in Early Childhood Learning settings in Dubai,Sharjah, and Ajman. We offer a personal teaching service just for your grade. We also conduct Holiday programs at different locations in Sharjah, Ajman and Dubai and we offer group sessions. Our online workshops are available.

We need minimal space and certain items that will be informed to you beforehand.
-Six clear tables for students to work at;
-A presentation table and floor space to accommodate the class in front;
-The use of a rubbish bin and electrical power point;
-Access to the venue 30 minutes prior and at the end of the session for setting-up and cleaning-up;
-Parking as close as possible to the venue;
-Some workshops require access to a tap and basin.
-We ask that teachers help with the supervision of sessions. You might be surprised with what you learn too – a perfect professional development opportunity!

Our classroom workshops align with leading international curriculums. Our topics cover physical, life, earth and space , simultaneously linked to math, technology, arts,and chemistry sciences.

If there is a topic of interest not listed on our website, please inquire as we are skilled at tailoring programs based on specific needs and requirements.
