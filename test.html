About Us

Our team of academic specialists truly care about connecting young boffins to the world of science and its wonders. We conduct non-hazardous offline science activities and experiments and online workshops with impressive demonstrations, safe hand on experiments that prove to be amazing take aways for primary and kindergarten learners.

Our courses

  • Physical Science

    Give wings to your imagination and explore the facts about forces such as friction, gravity, magnetism and more impact our everyday lives, as well as everything from atoms through to galaxies!

  • Chemical Science

    Learn about what is the effect of heating and cooling on thre states of matter, how surface tension works. Explore chemical reactions and understand how substances are formed.

  • Life Science

    Investigate microorganism. Learn how food in our body fuctions. Explore the difference between living and non-living things. Understand how living and non living things are interdependent on each other.

  • Earth and Space Science

    Explore the interconnection between land, weather, and natural disasters. Learn some exciting information about rocks, space, and sustainability.

  • STEM

    Stem skill and knowledge is imbedded in the all the leading curriculum. It is the most important part of everyday life. Explore how science, mathematics, Design and Technology can develop critical and creative thinking, collaboration, ethical decision making.
